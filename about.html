About Me

Design

Hi, I'm Vivian -

a multidisciplinary designer focusing on product design and user research, and a recent graduate from the University of Waterloo.

I am passionate about creating innovative products that speak to people through teamwork, research, and visual design. Research and design go hand in hand in product development. As a designer, I strive to design the right thing and to design things right.

Throughout my undergraduate I have studied how to leverage mathematical models and statistical analysis in UX research, been immersed in design projects inside and outside the classroom, and put pen to paper during my internship as part of a marketing and design team with University of Waterloo Food Services. I have even worn the entrepreneurship hat in several team projects researching and designing apps for students in the community. Along the way, I’ve learned the importance of understanding how people — clients and teammates — think, what they need, and how to fulfill it.
